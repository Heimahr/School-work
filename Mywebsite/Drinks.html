Wine culture

I would recommend a good quality white wine with most pasta, as it is common among italians to drink wine with food. Save red wine for meat.

You don't have to drink wine

Don't feel preassured to drink wine when eating italian food, not every italian drinks wine either, especially if you're just cooking for yourself.
