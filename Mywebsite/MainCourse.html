Main course

Pasta Carbonara

Ingredients

Here is what you need...

  • Fresh tagliatelle is the best, but normal spaghetti is fine
  • Eggs, 1 per person
  • Bacon, or traditional pancetta ham if you can get it
  • Cheese, usually pecorino and / or parmesan
  • Black pepper and Sea salt

Preparation

First you want to cut the bacon / ham and cook it on a pan on medium low temperature to release plenty of fat. Mix the egg and grate the cheese in a large bowl, then mix. Be generous. Finish the sauce with touch of a black pepper. If you're fast you can prepare the pasta while making the sauce, however make sure the sauce is ready once the pasta is ready. Once the pasta is ready pour the water out and mix the pasta with the bacon. Next add the sauce (cheesy egg mixture) on top, make sure the pan is off and cooled down a bit so the egg wont turn into scrambled paste once you add it. Mix well and enjoy!
