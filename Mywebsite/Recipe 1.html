Bruschetta

Bruschetta ingredients

here is what you need...

  • bread, preferably white
  • garlic
  • usually fresh tomato, tomato sauce will do
  • Cheese, usually mozzarella
  • other optional ingredients, ham for example

Preparation

First toast the bread slices in the oven to give them a crunchy surface, then finely mince garlic on top and add the toppings of your choice. Then put them back in the oven until the cheese melts.
